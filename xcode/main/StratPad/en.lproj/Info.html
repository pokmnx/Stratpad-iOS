About StratPad

StratPad was created to help businesses perform better. Period.

It simplifies business plans (and business planning). It helps you think differently, plan differently and manage differently.

StratPad was designed by Alex Glassey, a former big-5 consultant turned serial entrepreneur, professor and business advisor. "Entrepreneurs and small business owners should have access to world-class business tools that are tailored to their unique needs. That's why I created StratPad."

Thousands of businesses (and their consultants) have made StratPad the highest-rated business planning and strategy app in the world.

We hope you find it useful, too.
