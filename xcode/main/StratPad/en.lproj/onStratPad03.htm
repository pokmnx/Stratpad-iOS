Quick Start

Get Started Quickly
The fastest way to learn how StratPad works may be to open one of the three sample StratFiles, see how its information is entered, and view, print and email the reports that are created.

Follow these four steps:

1. Touch the StratFiles button and then touch on one of the three sample StratFiles to open it.
2. Walk through the StratFile. Start by touching the "StratFile" button in the Navigator. Then keep swiping.
3. View any of the nine reports at any time. Simply touch one of the report buttons. Swipe left and right to move between pages and reports.
4. Print or email a report by touching the toolbar.

The next screen introduces the three sample StratFiles. Swipe from right to left to see them.
