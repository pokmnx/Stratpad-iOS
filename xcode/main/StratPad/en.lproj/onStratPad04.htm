On StratPad

The 3 Sample StratFiles

Three sample StratFiles are included to start you off. Walk through them to get ideas for your own strategy. Feel free to experiment with them to get a sense of how StratPad works.

Acme Software Technologies Ltd.
Acme is a young software company. They intend to complete the development on their first product (DocLock) and start their sales and marketing efforts.

Acme's strategy is tightly focused and has a shorter timeframe than an older company.

Beta Metal Designs
Beta is an established firm serving a regional market in upstate New York. They now want to expand their service offerings and customer base while improving their internal processes.

Beta's strategy has more detail and a longer timeframe than Acme's.

Gamma Consulting
Gamma is a boutique consulting firm that offers general management advice to businesses. They've recently decided that narrowing their focus will help them differentiate themselves from their competition.

Their strategy is designed to take place over two years. It focuses on their sales and marketing objectives and metrics during this period.

View, Change, Email, Print
Remember that you can do anything you like with these sample StratFiles including changing them, emailing them to yourself and others, and printing them directly to a wireless printer.
