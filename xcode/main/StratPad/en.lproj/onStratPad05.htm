StratPad and Your Strategic Planning Process

Your strategic planning process should follow the steps below. You can learn more about the blue triangle from the detailed discussion in the "On Strategy" chapter.

  • in StratPad, review "On Strategy" and "Toolkit"
  • talk with key management and staff about your values and long-term aspirations
  • research your customers, competitors and landscape
  • discuss strategy elements and critical projects with management and staff
  • in StratPad, complete StratFile, Discussion, Strat Stmt., Themes and Theme Detail chapters
  • in StratPad, complete Objectives and Activity chapters after discussions with your team
  • use the reports frequently to ensure consistency and alignment
