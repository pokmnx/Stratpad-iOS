On Strategy

What is Strategy

Strategy is the topic that concerns itself with helping a company succeed in the medium- and long-term.

Strategy helps you understand how your company will fulfill its purpose and aspirations.

Strategy asks, "What makes you unique? How will you defend that uniqueness? How will you turn it to your advantage?"

Strategy demands that you take a position and make tough decisions in the face of uncertainty. It asks you to be intentional in your thinking.

In short, strategy helps you maximize your company's potential. It:

  • clarifies thinking
  • provides focus
  • guides decisions
  • aligns your people, and
  • builds brand equity and shareholder value.
