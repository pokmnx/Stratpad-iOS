On Strategy

Aspiration (2 of 2)

Ask yourself these questions as you think through your company's aspiration:

  • Why do we exist? What is our fundamental reason for being?
  • If our company were shut down, to whom would it matter and why?
  • What do we aspire to become, achieve, create?
  • It's 10 years in the future: what would we love to see? What should this company look like? What should it feel like to employees? What should it have achieved? If someone wrote an article for a major business magazine about this company, what would we want it to say?
  • Does our aspiration get our juices flowing? Do we find it stimulating? Does it spur us onward?

Think about writing one to three sentences on your company's aspiration by starting with "We want to become...".
