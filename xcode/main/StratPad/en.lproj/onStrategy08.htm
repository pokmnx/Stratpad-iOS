On Strategy

Values (1 of 2)

Your values walk hand-in-hand with your aspirations.

Values are what you stand for or believe in. They are deeply ingrained and guide all of your company's actions – for good or ill.

The best values are those that are created intentionally and are authentic. You embrace them and live them.

Values become a key source of your company's culture and distinctiveness. They are felt by your customers and therefore strongly influence your brand.

Apple's values might be:

  • We believe in the simple, not the complex.
  • We believe we need to own the primary technologies that we make and only participate in markets where we can make a significant contribution.
  • We believe in deep collaboration and cross-pollination of our groups.
  • We don't settle for anything less than excellence.
  • We admit when we are wrong and have the courage to change.
