Values (2 of 2)

Some of Amazon's values might be:

  • Customer Obsession: We start with the customer and work backwards.
  • Bias for Action: We live in a time of unheralded revolution and insurmountable opportunity – provided we make every minute count.
  • High Hiring Bar: When making a hiring decision we ask ourselves: "Will I admire this person? Will I learn from this person? Is this person a superstar?"
  • Frugality: We spend money on things that really matter and believe that frugality breeds resourcefulness, self-sufficiency, and invention!

What about YOUR values?
See if you can identify three to five values that your company currently holds or that you wish it held. These questions may help you better understand your values:

  • What values do we truly and passionately hold?
  • If the circumstances changed and penalized us for holding a value, would we keep it?
  • If we started a new company tomorrow, in a different line of work, what values would we want to build into that new company?
