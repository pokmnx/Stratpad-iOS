On Strategy

Scope (2 of 3)

Target Customers

  • What are the defining characteristics of our desired customers? Are they large or small? Near or far? Wealthy or not? Consumers or businesses? Ambitious or conservative?
  • What are their needs, wants and priorities? How are these changing?
  • Why do we want them as customers?

Edward Jones' customers are conservative, long-term individual investors who are not comfortable making serious financial decisions without the support of a trusted advisor.

Control

  • What do we develop, manage, own or otherwise control "in-house" versus what we source externally or perform with others?
  • Are these items aligned with our capabilities?

Edward Jones offers only third-party investments (as opposed to proprietary investment vehicles that they own or have developed).
