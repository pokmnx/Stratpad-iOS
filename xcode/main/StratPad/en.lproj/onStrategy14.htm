On Strategy

Scope (3 of 3)

Offerings

  • What products and/or services do we offer our customers?
  • Are they consistent with what our target customer wants/needs?
  • Do our offerings capture the full range of what our customers want? Do we care?
  • How will our offerings evolve to accommodate changing customer needs?

Edward Jones offers convenient, face-to-face financial advice along with third-party investments. They do not provide online trading.

By thinking about scope, you start to draw a clear picture of who you are along with sharp boundaries around what you do, what you offer and who you serve.

This picture becomes even clearer as you start to consider your advantage which is described on the next page.
