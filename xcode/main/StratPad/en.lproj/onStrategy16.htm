On Strategy

Customer Value and Capabilities (1 of 4)

Advantage stems from two fundamental attributes: customer value and your ability to create it (your capabilities).

Customer Value
Creating customer value comes from a deep understanding of your customers' needs and what contributes to their success.

Your customers want an offering that is:

  • meaningful. The offering has to make a significant difference to their lives.
  • sustainable. They need to trust that the offering can be consistently delivered.
  • distinctive. The offering is clearly different, in a substantive way, from the available alternatives.

Ask yourself:

  • What are the benefits of using our products/services?
  • Why should customers purchase our offering instead of an alternative?
  • Why are we the best option?
  • What do we want our customers to think, feel and experience?
  • What do we want our customers to say about us to others?
  • How can we prove that we will deliver the outcomes we say we can?
  • How can we get customers to think, "I believe you. I trust you."
