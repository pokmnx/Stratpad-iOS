Customer Value and Capabilities (2 of 4)

In short, potential buyers have to want and need what you're selling, believe that you can deliver on your promises, and see why you are superior to other options.

Capabilities
Carefully imagine your favorite restaurant. Think about all the things, large and small, that went into creating your positive experience of it including

the decor, comfort of chairs, appearance and attitude of staff, speed and quality of service, cleanliness of toilets, menu, quality and presentation of food, beverage list, location, parking, amenities, hours of operation, price...

This restaurant needed to bring together a variety of resources (building, furniture, food, staff) in combination with activities (menu design, cooking, hiring, training) to create a unique experience that delighted you.

These resources and activities are their capabilities.
