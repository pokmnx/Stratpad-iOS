On Strategy

Customer Value and Capabilities (3 of 4)

Now consider this:

What resources and/or activities will enable you, more than any other firm, to delight your target customer?

What people, technology, equipment, information, real estate, patents, channels, partnerships, culture, brand name and loyalties do you need? These are your resources.

What about your ability to design, develop, source, manufacture, market, hire and train staff, and service customers? These are your activities.

By clearly understanding what your customers value you will be able to identify the resources and activities that directly contribute to delighting your customers. These are your core capabilities.

Your success depends upon your ability to utilize your core capabilities consistently and well.
