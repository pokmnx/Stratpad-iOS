Final Thoughts on Advantage
As mentioned, your advantage is only an advantage if:

  • your customers are willing to pay for it, and/or
  • it gives you a distinct advantage over your competitors.

Your sustainable competitive advantage, therefore, grows out of:

  1. your understanding of your customer's needs and wants,
  2. the combination of activities and resources you assemble to meet those needs, and
  3. how well they’re managed.

This combination of customer-aligned activities and resources creates an "impenetrable thicket" that is difficult for a competitor to emulate.

This is your advantage.
