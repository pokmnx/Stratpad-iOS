On Strategy

Goal (2 of 2)

It's critically important that the goal you choose be measurable. In this way you can chart your progress and then celebrate when you arrive.

In addition, your goal must be driven by your aspiration and be consistent with your values.

There is likely no more important task for senior management than selecting an appropriate goal and communicating it effectively. For more discussion on this, see "Short Strategy Statements" in the "Toolkit".
