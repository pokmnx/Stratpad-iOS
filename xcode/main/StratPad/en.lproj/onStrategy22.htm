On Strategy

Scorecard

In order to succeed, strategy must link to execution. In other words, the theoretical must be made practical.

The linkage between strategy and execution must constantly be monitored so that you can:

  • Ensure that staff efforts continually support the strategic goal; and
  • Keep pace with changes in your world and adjust accordingly.

"If Soul provides the destination, and Strategy the map to get there, then Scorecard keeps us on track."
