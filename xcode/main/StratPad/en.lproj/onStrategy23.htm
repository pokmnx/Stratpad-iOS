Themes
A theme is a project that moves the organization toward its strategic goal.

For example, a strategic goal of "becoming the region's dominant integrated engineering design company in 3 years" might mean embarking on three projects (themes):

  1. Expand the current facility and add new equipment
  2. Improve production efficiency
  3. Increase marketing efforts
