Objectives

Objectives Think through each theme carefully by considering its objectives. You arrive at objectives by determining how each theme affects your company's finances, customers, processes/technology and staff.

For each theme, ask these questions:

  • How do we want this theme to financially impact our company?
  • What will our customers experience as a result of this theme?
  • What process and/or technology changes are needed to implement this theme?
  • How will we develop and engage our staff to fully support this theme?

Let's answer these questions for one theme for our example engineering firm:

Improve production efficiency
Financial impact: increased gross margin

Customers will experience: higher quality, faster turnaround

Process/technology changes: revised processes, updated admin systems

Staff development: fully trained staff, adjusted remuneration
