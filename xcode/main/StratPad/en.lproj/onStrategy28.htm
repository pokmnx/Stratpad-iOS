On Strategy

Scorecard, summarized

Strategy, by itself, goes nowhere. It must be operationalized.

  • Themes, or projects, are created to support the firm's strategic goal.
  • Objectives act as a checklist and ensure that the impact on finances, customers, process/technology, and staff is considered for each theme.
  • Metrics and activity for each objective are identified and then carefully monitored.

The next chapter, "Toolkit", will help you gain additional insight into your business and its strategic options.
