How to Use This Chapter

This chapter summarizes several tools that will provide additional insights into your business. They'll help you identify and prioritize options and, shared with your management team, will help foster understanding and create common ground.

Tools 1 and 2 provide an overview of our thinking. They remind you that leadership is directly connected to your customers' experience.

Tools 3 to 6 help you create uniqueness leading to stronger financial performance.

Tool 7 shows you how to craft a short strategy statement that helps you align your staff around a clear, common goal.
