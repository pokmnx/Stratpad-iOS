EntrepreneurialStrategy

EntrepreneurialStrategy is an adaptive, performance-oriented approach to business designed for owners and senior executives.

It arms business leaders with the essential elements of strategy, execution, leadership and brand in an accessible, practical format.

EntrepreneurialStrategy emphasizes four key points:

  1. Leadership is at the heart of strategy and execution;
  2. Strategy and execution are inextricably linked;
  3. Strategy is iterative; it must always be reviewed and adjusted regularly; and
  4. Your customers' experience is a direct result of your leadership, your strategy, and your ability to execute.
