EntrepreneurialStrategy

This page expands on the four points:

Leadership is at the heart of strategy and execution.
Strategy development requires unflinching assessment, frank dialogue and clear choice-making. It also requires courage to communicate clearly and act on one's convictions. Courageous leadership results in thoughtful and well-understood direction-setting.

Strategy and execution are inextricably linked.
This seems obvious. Too often, however, strategic direction is decided by one group with execution left to another. This dramatically decreases the likelihood of success.

Be sure to fully consider the operational, financial and human elements required to bring your strategy to life.

Strategy is iterative; it must always be reviewed and adjusted regularly.
Strategy isn't a weekend retreat. Strategy is a way of thinking and acting that must permeate your company. Even though strategy takes a longer-term view, it needs to adjust as the landscape changes.

Monitor the progress of your strategy frequently and revisit your strategy regularly.

Your customers' experience is a direct result of your leadership, your strategy, and your ability to execute.
When your leadership is clear, when your strategy is crisp and when your staff are aligned, your customers feel it and they respond by buying your offerings.

Be clear; be crisp; be aligned.
