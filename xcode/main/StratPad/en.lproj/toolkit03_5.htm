EntrepreneurialStrategy translates directly to StratPad:

EntrepreneurialStrategy

  • Assess Situation
  • Set Direction
  • Create Plans
  • Drive Results
  • Leadership
  • Iterate

StratPad

  • On Strategy, Toolkit, Discussion
  • Strategy Statement
  • Themes, Theme Detail, Objectives, Activity
  • Agenda report, StratBoard

Include your team (where practical and appropriate) in the strategic planning process; share results of the process with your team (consider, for example, sharing your short strategy statement with all staff); follow the Agenda faithfully; use StratBoard to monitor progress and share results; make adjustments as required.

Repeat the entire process at least once per year (and more frequently if a start-up).
