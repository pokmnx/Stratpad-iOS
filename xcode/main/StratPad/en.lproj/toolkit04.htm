Brand Equals...

"Brand" is an over-used word. Simply put, we believe that brand is the sum of all the experiences that your customer has with, and of, your firm.

Brand rests solely in the eyes of the customers. You can influence it but you cannot control it.

A customer's experience WITH your firm includes:

  • the voice of the person answering your phone;
  • your responsiveness;
  • your return policy;
  • the cleanliness of your bathroom;
  • your website;
  • your pricing;
  • the design of your product;
  • the quality of your product;
  • your product's packaging;
  • your marketing;
  • and everything else.

A customer's experience OF your firm includes reviews and word of mouth.

One of the best things you can do for your brand is to practice thinking like your customers.

Never forget this: Brand is equal to the sum of your customers' exeriences and perceptions.
