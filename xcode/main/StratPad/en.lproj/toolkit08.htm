The Sweet Spot

The Sweet Spot diagram has 8 regions:

Regions 1 & 2: This is the Sweet Spot. Your target customer is willing to pay for offerings in this region.

Region 1 is your Super Sweet Spot: your customer perceives your offering as unique and is willing to pay for it. Price pressure is reduced.

Region 2 is affected by competitive offerings: your price (and gross profit) may be squeezed.

Strategically, you’ll likely want to defend these areas and even expand them.

In Region 3, your customer has identified a need but has been unable to find a suitable supplier.

Region 4 identifies products or services that your target customer is not willing to pay for.
