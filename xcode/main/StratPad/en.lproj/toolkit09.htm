The Sweet Spot

Region 5 is your competitor’s "super sweet spot". They are free to sell their offerings unopposed.

In Region 6, you and your competitors have offerings that your target customers are not willing to pay for.

Region 7 identifies an area where neither you nor your competitors have offerings and where your target customers haven't identified a need. This could be the future: are your customers moving in that direction? Could you develop an offering that anticipates their needs? Doing so successfully could expand your Super Sweet Spot.

Finally, Region 8 identifies competitor offerings that you don't match and that your target customer is unwilling to pay for.
