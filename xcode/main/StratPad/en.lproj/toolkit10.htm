The Sweet Spot

This model should prompt many questions. Here are a few:

  • How can we further differentiate our region 2 offerings (and move them into region 1)?
  • Why is our target customer unwilling to pay for our region 4 and 6 offerings? Should we discontinue them?
  • Which region 3 offerings should we consider developing?
  • Which competitive offerings (especially in regions 2 and 5) should we be concerned about? Should we add or strengthen our offerings there or consider abandoning those regions?
  • Why does our competitor have an offering in region 8? Do they know something we don’t?
