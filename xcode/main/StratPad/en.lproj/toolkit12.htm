Expanding Profitability

By extending this model, we can identify seven fundamental ways to think about increasing profitability:

  1. Increase your customer’s willingness to pay. More features, better service, higher quality, marketing.
  2. If there is a gap between your customer’s willingness to pay and your price, then consider raising your price.
  3. Reduce your internal costs. Salaries, phones, rent, professional fees.
  4. Increase asset utilization. Be more efficient with what you have.
