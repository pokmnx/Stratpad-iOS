Expanding Profitability

  1. Pay suppliers less. In other words, reduce your willingness to pay.
  2. Help your suppliers reduce their costs and then share this cost reduction with them.
  3. Expand your market size.

Use these topics to start discussions among your management and staff.

Consider approaching your suppliers and customers with some of these topics. This can create "win-win" discussions and are excellent ways to create "super sweet spot" offerings.
