Gross Profit vs. Net Income

The bottom line on the call center story? Consider focusing on gross profit. Net income will follow. Here are five ways to increase gross profit:

  1. Increase revenue by examining opportunities to raise prices.
  2. Optimize cost of goods sold by evaluating efficiency and utilization options.
  3. Invest in sales and marketing efforts that increase revenues in the short- and medium-term.
  4. Invest in research and development activities that increase revenues in the medium- and long-term.
  5. Evaluate general and administrative items for investment options that affect revenue or cost of goods sold.
