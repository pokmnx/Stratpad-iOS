How to use StratPad

StratPad helps you learn about strategy, create your own strategy, and then execute your strategy. Here's how:

Learn
The first four chapters teach you basic strategy concepts and how to use StratPad. In the Navigator (on the left of the screen) the Learn chapters start with lower-case roman numerals (i, ii, iii and iv).

Create
Use the next chapters (numbered from 1 to 7) to think about and create the strategic aspects of your company.

StratPad becomes your personal business advisor and guides you through the process.

Share
StratPad takes your plan and automatically creates nine reports. Use them to refine, share and execute your strategy. View these reports by tapping R1 to R9 in the Navigator at any time.

Monitor
Use StratPad to keep your team on track. StratPad graphs the actual values of your metrics along with their targets and trend-lines.

The next page shows you how to move around StratPad. Swipe from right to left.
